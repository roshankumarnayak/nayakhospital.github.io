prof. Manish Kumar Meher

Prosthodontist with specialization in all aspects
of fixed and removable oral & maxillofacial prosthesis. She is trained and efficient in
providing health care benefits for people of all age groups.
She completed her BDS from Institute of Dental Studies and Technologies, Modinagar,
Meerut in 2013 and her MDS from Government Dental College and Hospital, Cuttack,
Odisha in 2017. She was also associated with NIT, Rourkela as a Clinical Research
Fellow for the duration of her thesis during her post graduation. After completing
her post graduation she worked as a consultant to general and other practicing dentists
in Bangalore. While working with Clove Dental, India's largest dental chain, she was the
Clinic Head for the Kumaraswamy Layout branch for 2 years.
She has been sworn in by the Hippocratic Oath to maintain an ethical practice,
keeping in mind only the well being of her patients, which she carries out diligently.
She is committed to serve her patients to the best of her capabilities and aims to keep
evolving in her field to provide an ethical and a comprehensive oral health care.
